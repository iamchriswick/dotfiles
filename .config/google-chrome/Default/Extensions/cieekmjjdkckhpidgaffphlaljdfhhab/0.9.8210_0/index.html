Welcome to Mindful

A space for your thoughts, ideas and ongoing tasks

Mindful helps you stay organized, take notes, keep track of your to-dos, or draft an article or email.

It works where you work. In every new tab.

“Start a new line with SPACE key for flow-menu.”
  • ⌘ + B for bold
  • ⌘ + i for italic
  • ⌘ + k for link
  • ⌘ + 5 for strikethrough
  • ⌘ + . to tick off a checkbox
  • ⇧ + ⌘ + . to remove checkboxes

“Happy writing.” :)
